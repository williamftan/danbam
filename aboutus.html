DanBam Story

DanBam is a Korean restaurant that was founded in 2005 in Korea. Thanks to DanBam's success in Korea, DanBam expanded its business in 2015 to various regions, including Indonesia. In Indonesia, DanBam has opened 3 branches, namely in Jakarta, Bekasi, and also Bogor.

Even though the branch is outside Korea, DanBam always maintains the quality of its food ingredients by importing the ingredients and seasonings directly from the branch in Korea.

The Soul of DanBam

DanBam is a Korean restaurant serving traditional Korean food packaged in a modern way.

DanBam provides the best place for dine-in and the best packaging for take-away.

DanBam always maintains the quality of service because customer satisfaction is DanBam's pleasure
